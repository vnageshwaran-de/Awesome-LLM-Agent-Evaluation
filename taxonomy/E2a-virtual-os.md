# E2a — Virtual operating systems

**Pillar:** III — WHERE it is tested (Environment Topologies; dynamic E2)

**Definition.** Sandboxed OS environments (containers or VMs exposing a filesystem, shell, and applications) for system-administration, file-manipulation, and desktop-application tasks.

**Strength.** High-fidelity programmatic scoring (H1) — success conditions assertable against machine state.

**Limitation.** Operational cost and environment-setup fragility; fidelity to a real user's machine is itself a validity variable.

**Benchmarks:** OSWorld (Ubuntu/Windows/macOS VMs); AgentBench (OS environment).
