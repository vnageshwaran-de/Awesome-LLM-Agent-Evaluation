## 3. Recall-gap quantification (RQ7) — labelled `estimate`, never `verified`

- **Probe set:** 10 source-verified canonical benchmarks (AgentBench, SWE-bench, GAIA, AppWorld, OSWorld, MLE-bench, DataSciBench, AgentBoard, VisualWebArena, ToolEmu). WebArena, ToolBench, τ-bench, and three others failed conservative auto-verification and were excluded from the frozen set.
- **Step 1 — probe recall:** OpenAlex **10/10 (1.00)**; **Scopus 9/10 (0.90)**; Crossref **4/10 (0.40)**; ScienceDirect **0/10**; IEEE Xplore **0/10**; ACM DL **0/10**.
- **Scopus is the key new evidence:** unlike IEEE/ACM/ScienceDirect (which host only citing/derivative works), Scopus indexes the **canonical benchmark paper itself** for 9 of 10 probes (TITLE-scoped, verbatim title matches confirmed); only DataSciBench (a Feb-2025 preprint) is missing. The 2×2 with OpenAlex is both=9, OA-only=1, **Scopus-only=0**.
- **Probe gap vs. union of ALL 6 sources = 0.0** — every probe benchmark any source hosts is in OpenAlex. Scopus, the one independent index that *does* carry the canonical papers, adds zero that OpenAlex lacked. This independently confirms the Crossref capture–recapture value (~7.7%) was a heterogeneity artifact, not a real recall figure.
- **IEEE registration:** 962, field scoping honored → `count_only_verified`. **ACM registration:** 17,231 but `broad_expansion` (interface degraded Abstract:/Keyword: to all-field; reported as over-broad, NOT field-restricted). Capture–recapture declined for both (m=0, same-population violated). Evidence: `data/ieee_acm_tier2.json`.
- **ScienceDirect registration:** 785 field-scoped results (TAK, 2023–2026, scoping honored — not `broad_expansion`). Capture–recapture vs. SD **declined**: m=0 and the same-population assumption is violated. Bot-detection episode during the first rapid probe pass logged with evidence; values come from a slow re-run, no bypass (`data/sciencedirect_tier2.json`).
- **Step 2 — 2×2 (OpenAlex vs Crossref) on probe set:** both=4, OpenAlex-only=6, Crossref-only=0, neither=0.
- **Step 3 — Chapman capture–recapture:** n_OA=259, n_CR=219, m=16 → **N̂ ≈ 3364**, implied OpenAlex recall ≈ **0.077**.
  - Reported as an **order-of-magnitude lower bound**, with three stated assumptions. The two engines are heterogeneous targeted searches (not independent random samples) and Crossref sampling was depth-capped, which **depresses m and inflates N̂**, biasing implied recall downward. It is contradicted — reassuringly — by the 100% probe capture.
- **Synthesis:** recall on known/influential benchmarks is effectively complete; long-tail recall is materially below 100% and not precisely estimable here.
